Core Values of COC

Cagayan de Oro College, or COC, is noted for upholding its core values of competence, integrity, openness, patriotism, and devotion. These principles are deeply embedded in the organization and manifest themselves in the daily conduct of its faculty, staff, and students. These values establish COC's identity, guide its members, and motivate it to strive for excellence and meaningful influence.

Why Marketing Management?

Marketing management is the art and method of comprehending people's requirements and desires and figuring out how to effectively reach them. It's not only about making sales. It all comes down to connecting. It all boils down to knowing your audience, telling a compelling story, and building credibility over time.

The goal of marketing management is to add value for consumers while supporting business growth. It's about making a profit while keeping the customer in mind.

Student Journey

Jerry & Jaycee’s Reflections

Our journey at PHINMA COC has been filled with growth, laughter, and meaningful experiences. From the very first day, we felt welcomed by the community—everyone, from the instructors to our fellow students, made it easy to feel at home.

One of the moments we’ll always remember was during a campus event where we showcased our creativity and bonded with other students. It was more than just fun—it reminded us that learning doesn’t only happen in the classroom. It happens in the friendships we build and the confidence we gain from trying new things.

As BSBA students, we’ve been challenged to think critically and apply what we’ve learned in real-world scenarios. The lessons here aren’t just academic—they’re life lessons that will shape our future. The instructors don’t just teach; they guide and encourage us every step of the way.

We’re also grateful to be scholarship recipients. Having a portion of our tuition covered has helped us focus more on our studies and personal growth without the financial stress.

PHINMA COC is more than just a school to us—it’s a place where we’ve discovered our potential, found true friends, and built a clearer vision of the future we want to achieve.
